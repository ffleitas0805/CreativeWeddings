Our
History

Since 1990, Creative Weddings has become the local and international destination
for distinctive wedding invitations, bridal accessories, favors, and headpieces. Our
luxurious store of 32 years is located on the famed Miracle Mile in Coral Gables, one
of the wedding capitals of the world, just minutes away from Downtown Miami.

Our
Recognition

The unsurpassed experience, selection and creativity combined with the elegant store
environment and comfortable seating in our invitation room has earned us accolades as
one of the top wedding stores in the Americas.

Our
International Presence

Along with loyal South Floridian clientele, our proficiency in Spanish and international service
attracts clients all through the Caribbean, Central and South America, where we make the otherwise
impossible, possible for brides who want the finest things for their wedding not available in their country.
